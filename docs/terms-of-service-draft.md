# Central Stadium 360 — Terms of Service (DRAFT v0.1)

_Draft for legal review. Benchmarked against VOO (voo.mn), ORI (ori.mn), LookTV (looktv.mn), Showbox, and DAZN. Governing law: Mongolia. Currency: MNT (₮)._

> **⚠️ Not legal advice.** This is an engineering-drafted starting point built from public/benchmark research. Have Mongolian counsel review before publishing — especially payment, refund, and data-protection clauses (Mongolia's Law on Personal Data Protection, 2021/in force 2022).

## Benchmark takeaways
- **DAZN** (closest live-sports analog): 3 registered devices, 2 concurrent streams, "account is personal and cannot be shared," same-network enforcement, fees generally non-refundable. → validates our 1/3/5 tiered device model and anti-sharing enforcement.
- **LookTV**: 96-hour catch-up window. **ORI**: catch-up on 5 of 20 channels + a "Ticket" rental model. → time-boxed catch-up (our "within July" replay window) is an established local norm.
- **VOO**: personal/non-commercial use only, commercial use = immediate termination; package change once per month; 5-day notice for terms changes.
- **Showbox**: generic boilerplate only (piracy-adjacent) — structural template, not a compliance model.
